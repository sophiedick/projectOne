BrainSmart 2.0 This is a game for 2 players designed to test both your memory and concentration levels. There are 4 lists. Each list will populate individually and you will have 10 seconds to memorize all 7 items in the list. After 40 seconds all the lists will have viewed. A random generator will then ask you to list all the items in ONE of the 4 lists. The player who remembers the most items is the winner!
